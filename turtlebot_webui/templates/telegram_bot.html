💬Chat ID Configuration

💡 How to get your Chat ID:
1. Start a chat with your bot
2. Send any message to the bot
3. Visit: https://api.telegram.org/bot[YOUR_BOT_TOKEN]/getUpdates
4. Look for "chat":{"id": YOUR_CHAT_ID}
